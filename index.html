Here is some stuff that's happened to them:

  • 2011 - McCafferty was formed & Nick Harktop realeased Mom+Dads EP
  • 2012 - Harktop released Japan and DanceBeats To Hurt Girls
  • 2013 - I Hate This Body, It's a Bad Idea, Forest Life and This Will Mean Nothing were released. Band signed with Monkey Boy Records + released Beachboy later in the year.
  • 2015 - Released two more EPs: When The Lightning Hit and Happy Birthday, Dad
  • 2016 - Harktop decided to end McCafferty, but later came back after some successful crowdfunding to support another EP.
  • 2017 - McCafferty signed with Take This To Heart Records + released Thanks. Sorry. Sure.
  • 2018 - Released a split EP with Heart Attack Man + signed to Triple Crown Records.
  • * Next album Yarn set to be released at the end of March 2018.

"I started as a very basic level musician and then slowly progressed into the artist I am today. The new album is going to be very very very cool."

McCafferty Vocalist Nick Harktop
